The Property Record

Your home has a history.
Now it has a record.

A vehicle history report tells you what happened to a car before you bought it. Your MantaRay Property Record does the same for your home — giving every party in a transaction, an insurance review, or a refinance a verified, professionally documented account of your property's true condition over time.

We inspect it. We document it. We archive it permanently in your secure client portal. Every repair logged, every upgrade recorded, every inspection added to a growing history that becomes more valuable with every entry. When it's time to sell, refinance, or renew your coverage, your property record is ready to share with a single link.
